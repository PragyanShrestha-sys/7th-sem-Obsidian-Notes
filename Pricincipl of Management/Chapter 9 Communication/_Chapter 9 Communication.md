## [[Concept - Structure and process of communication]]

| Concept                    | Key Takeaways                                                                            |
| :------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Definition**             | Exchange of info + understanding between sender and receiver.                            |
| **Process (8 Steps)**      | Sender → Encodes → Message → Channel → Decodes → Receiver + Feedback + Noise.            |
| **Directions (Structure)** | Downward (Top-down), Upward (Bottom-up), Horizontal (Peer), Diagonal (Cross-functional). |
| **Formal vs. Informal**    | Formal = Official channels. Informal = Grapevine (rumors/gossip).                        |
| **7 Cs of Communication**  | Clear, Concise, Correct, Complete, Concrete, Considerate, Courteous.                     |
