## [[Concept of control and quality management]]

 *"Control is the process of checking whether proper progress is being made towards the objectives and goals, and acting if necessary to correct any deviation."* – **Koontz & O'Donnell**

**In simple words:** Quality Management is the process of ensuring that an organization's products, services, and processes consistently meet or exceed customer expectations. 

It is not just about "checking" quality at the end of the production line; it is about *building* quality into every single step of the process.

## [[Quality Control Systems]]


If a general **control system** is about checking *everything* (profits, sales, employee attendance, etc.), a **Quality Control System** is hyper-focused on one specific, critical area: **making sure the product or service is EXCELLENT.** 

In simple words: **A Quality Control System is the specific process of inspecting, testing, and checking products or services to ensure they meet the established quality standards, and immediately fixing them if they don’t.**
