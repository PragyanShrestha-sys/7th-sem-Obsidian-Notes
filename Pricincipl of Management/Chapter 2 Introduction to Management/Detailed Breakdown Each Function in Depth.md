### Planning

| Type of Plan | Time Horizon | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategic plan | 3–10 years | Enter two new international markets. |
| Tactical plan | 1–2 years | Open 5 new stores in City X. |
| Operational plan | Weekly/daily | Schedule 10 cashiers for Saturday. |

### Organizing

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Departmentalization | Group jobs by function, product, geography, customer. |
| Span of control | How many subordinates a manager supervises (narrow vs. wide). |
| Chain of command | Formal line of authority. |
| Delegation | Assigning authority and responsibility to others. |

### Leading

| Leadership Style | When Effective |
| --- | --- |
| Autocratic | Crisis, new untrained employees. |
| Democratic | Skilled workers, need buy-in. |
| Laissez-faire | Highly expert, self-motivated teams. |

### Controlling

| Control Type | Timing | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feedforward | Before work begins | Inspect raw materials. |
| Concurrent | During work | Monitor assembly line quality. |
| Feedback | After work completed | Customer satisfaction survey. |
