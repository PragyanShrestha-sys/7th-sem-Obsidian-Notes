[[Differnece between K means and K Medoids]]

**In simple words:**

- **K-Means** = Creates an "imaginary center" (average). Fast but gets easily ruined by outliers.
- **K-Medoids** = Picks a "real existing point" as the center. Slower but handles outliers much better and works with any type of data.
