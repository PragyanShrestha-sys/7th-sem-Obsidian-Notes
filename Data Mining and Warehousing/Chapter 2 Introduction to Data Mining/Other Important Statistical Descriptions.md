### 1. Frequency Distribution
Shows how often each value or value range appears.

| Age Range | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- |
| 10-20 years | 5 |
| 20-30 years | 12 |
| 30-40 years | 8 |

### 2. Percentiles and Quartiles
- **Percentile:** Value below which a given percentage falls (e.g., 90th percentile = 90% of data is below this)
- **Quartiles:** Percentiles dividing data into 4 equal parts (Q1=25th, Q2=50th/Median, Q3=75th)

### 3. Correlation
Measures the relationship between two numeric attributes.

| Correlation value | Meaning                                                     |
| :---------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- |
| +1                | Perfect positive relationship (as X increases, Y increases) |
| 0                 | No relationship                                             |
| -1                | Perfect negative relationship (as X increases, Y decreases) |
