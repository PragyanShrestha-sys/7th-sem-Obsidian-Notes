[[Baysian classificatin Theory explanatin]] :Bayesian Classification calculates: "Given these attributes (features), what is the probability that this record belongs to each possible class?"

note: The reason Bayesian classification is powerful is not just that it gives probabilities – but that it gives **accurate** probabilities even with noisy or limited data. That's why spam filters, fraud detectors, and medical diagnosis systems use it daily.
