## [[Theory]]

The **Apriori algorithm** is ==a foundational data mining technique used to find frequent patterns, associations, or correlations among sets of items in large transactional databases==. It is famously used for **market basket analysis**, helping retailers identify products that customers often buy together—like bread and butter.
