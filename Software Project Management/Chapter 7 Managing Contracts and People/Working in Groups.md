
## Types of Formal Groups

Formal groups in an organization are divided into two main types:

| Type | Basis | Description | Software Example |
|------|-------|-------------|------------------|
| **Command Groups** | Organizational structure | Shown in organizational hierarchy charts; permanent or long-lasting groups based on reporting relationships. | Development team, Testing team, QA team |
| **Task Groups** | Specific tasks or projects | Formed to perform a particular project or task; members are selected from different command groups; temporary in nature. | A cross-functional team formed to develop a specific software module (e.g., payment gateway integration) |

---

## Key Takeaway

> Software projects need groups, but group work can be difficult. Managers must recognize the difference between **command groups** (ongoing, structure-based) and **task groups** (temporary, project-based) to organize teams effectively and reduce coordination problems.

| Stage          | Manager's Role                                                                                                                              |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Forming**    | Provide clear direction, roles, and project goals. Be highly directive.                                                                     |
| **Storming**   | Facilitate conflict resolution, encourage open communication, and establish ground rules. Don't suppress conflict—manage it constructively. |
| **Norming**    | Step back gradually; support team-developed norms and celebrate early wins.                                                                 |
| **Performing** | Act as a servant leader. Remove obstacles, but let the team self-manage.                                                                    |
| **Adjourning** | Acknowledge contributions, conduct retrospectives, and help team members transition to new projects.                                        |
