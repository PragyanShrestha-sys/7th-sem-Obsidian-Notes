Based on the image, here is an explanation of the **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** :

The WBS is a hierarchical breakdown of a project where the **root** is the **project name**. Using a **top-down approach**, each node is recursively broken into smaller sub-activities until the tasks at the leaf level become **undividable and independent**—meaning they cannot be broken down further and can be assigned or estimated individually.

### Summary Steps:
1. Identify the **major activities** of the project.
2. Break each major activity into **sub-activities**.
3. **Repeat** until all activities are simple, undividable, and independent.
