## What is SEI (Software Engineering Institute)?

The **Software Engineering Institute (SEI)** is a **research and development center** that focuses on advancing the practice of software engineering and improving the quality of software-intensive systems.

- **Founded:** 1984
- **Role:** Has been instrumental in advancing software engineering research and education

## [[What is SEI Capability Maturity Model (CMM)]]

The **Capability Maturity Model (CMM)** is a **process maturity framework** that assesses the sophistication of an organization's software development processes. It defines five evolutionary levels, each representing a **plateau of process capability** that enables predictable, high-quality software outcomes.
