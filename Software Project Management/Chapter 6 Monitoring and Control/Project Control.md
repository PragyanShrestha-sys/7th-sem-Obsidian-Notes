## What is Project Control?

Project Control is the process of **monitoring, measuring, and regulating** the progress and performance of a project to ensure it achieves its objectives within defined constraints.

## Key Constraints Monitored

|Constraint|Description|
|---|---|
|Time|Schedule adherence and milestone completion|
|Cost|Budget tracking and expenditure control|
|Scope|Managing deliverables and preventing scope creep|
|Quality|Ensuring outputs meet required standards|
|Risk|Identifying and mitigating potential issues|
|Resources|Optimizing allocation of people, tools, and equipment|

## Purpose of Project Control

- Compares actual progress against the planned baseline
- Identifies deviations and variances early
- Enables corrective actions to realign the project
- Balances competing constraints to ensure successful completion
- Provides stakeholders with accurate status reports

## Features and Benefits of Project contorol

## Features of Project Control

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Applied at Every Level | Top-down process where management sets strategy, teams execute at operational level |
| Ongoing Process | Continuous activity throughout the project lifecycle |
| Regular Feedback | Constant comparison of actual vs. planned performance |
| Flexible | Adaptable to project needs and changing conditions |



## Benefits of Project Control

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Optimized Resource Utilization | Ensures efficient use of time, money, and manpower |
| Facilitates Effective Decision Making | Enables timely, informed decisions to correct deviations |
| Improved Accountability | Establishes clear responsibilities among team members |
| Better Coordination and Transparency | Improves communication and early problem identification |
| Regular Monitoring and Reporting | Keeps stakeholders informed of project status |
| Prevents Gold Plating and Scope Creep | Ensures all changes are evaluated against original plan |

## Key Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Gold Plating | Adding unnecessary features not requested by client |
| Scope Creep | Uncontrolled expansion of project scope without approval |
